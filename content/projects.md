        ## Research

        <ul class="dz-entry-list">

          <li>
            <p class="dz-entry-title">T-DIET: longitudinal dietary patterns and health outcomes</p>
            <p class="dz-entry-meta">PhD project · Trinity College Dublin · 2025 – present</p>
            <p>
              Statistical methods for modelling how dietary patterns change over time and how they
              relate to health outcomes. Supervised by
              <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/silviadangelo/home?authuser=0" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Assistant Professor Silvia D'Angelo</a>
              and funded by the Research Ireland Frontiers for the Future Programme.
            </p>
          </li>

          <li>
            <p class="dz-entry-title">Large language models and sequential decision-making</p>
            <p class="dz-entry-meta">MSc project · AIMS South Africa / Stellenbosch University · Mar – Jun 2025 · Distinction</p>
            <p>
              Tested how well large language models solve sequential decision-making problems, using
              the multi-armed bandit problem as the main setting. The project compared LLM behaviour
              against classical bandit algorithms and looked at where the models fail when they have
              to reason under uncertainty over many rounds. Supervised by
              <a href="https://mila.quebec/en/directory/audrey-durand" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Prof Audrey Durand</a>,
              Université Laval.
            </p>
          </li>

          <li>
            <p class="dz-entry-title">Key relay protocol for quantum key distribution</p>
            <p class="dz-entry-meta">g-RIPS-Sendai · Tohoku University · Jun – Aug 2025</p>
            <p>
              Studied key relay protocols for quantum key distribution at the g-RIPS-Sendai
              programme, run jointly by IPAM (UCLA), AIMR, and Tohoku University in Japan, with
              Mitsubishi Electric as the industry partner.
            </p>
            <p>
              <a class="dz-pub-link" href="https://www.mccs.tohoku.ac.jp/g-rips/report/2025/pdf/Mitsubishi%20B_final_report.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">[report]</a>
