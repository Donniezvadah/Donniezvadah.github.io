T-DIET: longitudinal dietary patterns and health outcomes

PhD project · Trinity College Dublin · 2025 – present

Statistical methods for modelling how dietary patterns change over time and how they relate to health outcomes. Supervised by Assistant Professor Silvia D'Angelo and funded by the Research Ireland Frontiers for the Future Programme.

Large language models and sequential decision-making

MSc project · AIMS South Africa / Stellenbosch · Mar – Jun 2025 · Distinction

Tested how well large language models solve sequential decision-making problems, using the multi-armed bandit problem as the main setting. Compared LLM behaviour against classical bandit algorithms and examined where the models fail when reasoning under uncertainty over many rounds. Supervised by Prof Audrey Durand, Université Laval.

Large language models Multi-armed bandits
