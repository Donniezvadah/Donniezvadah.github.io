            <p class="dz-box-title">Large language models and sequential decision-making</p>
            <p class="dz-box-meta">MSc project · AIMS South Africa / Stellenbosch · Mar – Jun 2025 · Distinction</p>
            <p>
              Tested how well large language models solve sequential decision-making problems, using
              the multi-armed bandit problem as the main setting. Compared LLM behaviour against
              classical bandit algorithms and examined where the models fail when reasoning under
              uncertainty over many rounds. Supervised by
              <a href="https://mila.quebec/en/directory/audrey-durand" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Prof Audrey Durand</a>,
              Université Laval.
            </p>
